# Data Sources

## Overview
The warehouse integrates three core source domains. Each domain is ingested into the raw layer and then passed through validation and standardization before loading into the analytical database.

**Data range:** 2020-01-01 to 2025-12-31 (6 years)
**Regions:** Nairobi, Eldoret, Kisumu, Meru, Nakuru
**Products:** maize, beans, tomatoes, potatoes, wheat

## Integration Rules
- All datasets align on `date` as the primary join key
- Regional naming is consistent across all sources (title case: `Nairobi` etc.)
- Product naming in raw files is **lowercase** — must be standardized during staging
- All three datasets must be conformed to the warehouse grain of `date + product + region` before loading

## Transformation Notes
- **Weather** is at the coarser `date + region` grain — it must be fanned out to `date + product + region` during staging to support fact table joins
- **Production** snapshots are taken every 10 days — interpolation or forward-fill may be applied during warehouse load if daily granularity is needed
- **Market prices** are daily and directly match the warehouse grain

## Source Generation
Raw files were generated by `scripts/generate_raw_data.py` using realistic seasonal patterns, regional climate profiles, and annual price drift to simulate real agricultural data.
